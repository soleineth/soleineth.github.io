I am a 23-year-old Frenchwoman who recently graduated from Sciences Po Paris, France.

I moved to Gainesville, Florida during the summer 2015 to begin a new entrepreneurial journey with my husband. Together, we founded Mapn with the desire to help small businesses develop customer-targeted exposure using video advertising. I was excited to lead all design aspects of our startup, from drafting our initial UI by hand to crafting inviting social media posts and engaging presentations. Working with UF students to develop our web-app sparked my interest for the fields of UI and UX Design. I am now looking to continue to grow my knowledge and expertise of the fields and their context in the tech industry at large by joining a vigorous design team.
